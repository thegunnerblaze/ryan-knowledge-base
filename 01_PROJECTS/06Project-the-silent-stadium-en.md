## 2ï¸âƒ£ Problem â€” The Issue This Game Addresses

I am a quiet and introverted person.

Even when I have many thoughts, interests, and emotions inside me, they rarely come out naturally.  
Because of this, people often see only silence, not what exists beneath it.

This problem appears most strongly when:

* I want to express myself but cannot find the right words
* I feel pressure to explain myself clearly and quickly
* Silence is misunderstood as emptiness

For introverted people, this problem is especially difficult because:

* Inner richness is often invisible
* Being quiet is easily mistaken for having nothing to say
* Explaining oneself can feel exhausting or unnatural

This project begins with one question:

> **What if my inner self could be experienced as a place, rather than explained with words?**

## 6ï¸âƒ£ Game Structure

### Entry

* The player spawns outside a soccer stadium
* They stand in front of a **ticket booth**
* The player must **purchase a ticket** to enter

The ticket symbolizes permission to enter my inner world.

### Progression

* The player enters the stadium
* The green field is **not** the exhibition space
* Instead, the player walks clockwise along the **circular outer ring**
* Exhibitions are encountered one by one in sequence

### Ending

* After viewing all exhibitions, the player exits the stadium
* At that exact moment, a large crowd enters
* The player and the crowd cross paths
* The game ends
