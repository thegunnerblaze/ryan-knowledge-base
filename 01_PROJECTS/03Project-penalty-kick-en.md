## 5ï¸âƒ£ Solution â€” My Proposed Approach

I created a watercolor painting titled **Penalty Kick**, using the most tense moment in soccer to mirror the urgency of air pollution.

Key ideas:

* A goalkeeper wearing a gas mask represents survival in a polluted world
* Reflections in the mask lenses show factories and smoke
* The green soccer field contrasts sharply with the gray, polluted sky

The visual tension forces the viewer to confront pollution as something immediate and unavoidable.
